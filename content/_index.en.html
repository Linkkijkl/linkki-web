Welcome to Linkki's Homepage

Linkki Jyväskylä ry, more commonly known as Linkki, is a sizeable student association for Computer Science, Mathematical Information Technology, and Educational Technology majors and minors in University of Jyväskylä. Association was founded back in 2006 and it operates in the Faculty of Information Technology, within the Student Union of the University of Jyväskylä.
