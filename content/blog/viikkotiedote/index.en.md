## Sauna evening on the 27th of January

The first sauna evening of spring will be held at Opinkivi on January 27th from 4pm onwards!

To use the sauna and hot tub you must have a swimsuit with you. Snacks along with sparkling water and soda will be served at the sauna. The floors will be quite wet from people walking between the sauna and the hot tub, so make sure to bring a pair of flip flops or something of the like.
