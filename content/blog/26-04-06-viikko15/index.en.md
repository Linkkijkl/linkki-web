## Hat party w/ Tiltti 9.4. from 6pm to 11pm @ Opinkivi
What does molut taste like? How a bout a mix between sparkling wine and rum? Come to the Opinkivi sauna on April 9 from 6:00 PM to 11:00 PM to pick your own combination. Everyone is welcome to enjoy the sauna and spend the evening with the Tiltti crew and Linkkari folks, but if you want to join the 🎩 hat function 🎩, bring something drinkable—good or bad, with or without alcohol. Swimsuits are required in the sauna and hot tub.  🩳👙

**TLDR**:
**WHAT**: Tiltti x Linkki HAT PARTY🥳🎉🎉
**WHERE**: Opinkivi
**WHEN**: 9.4.2026 from 6pm to 11pm
**HOW MUCH**: The event is free of charge!
**WHAT TO BRING WITH**: Drink for the hat function, either good or bad, alcoholic or non alcoholic. Also bring your own bottle, sandals for the shower and hot tub and a swim suit!
