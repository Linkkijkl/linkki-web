# Events
## Pörssi's traditional Rinnerieha 12.3. from 2:00pm onwards @ Laajavuori
❄️ ARE YOU READY? RINNERIEHA IS BACK! ❄️

It’s time to turn our eyes toward the upcoming spring as Pörssi’s traditional Rinnerieha returns on March 12, 2026. On this day, Jyväskylä will be filled with colorful overalls as students from all across Finland gather to celebrate this unforgettable winter event.

The day kicks off with the slope activities at Laajavuori Ski Resort, where you’ll get to complete fun checkpoints and take part in various outdoor activities. After the slope session, it’s time to head to the official afterparty at London.

**Note! Tickets bought from Linkki's quota can be exchanged for wristband and overall patch at Kattila from a member of the board!**

TL;DR:
**What**: Rinnerieha
**Where**: Laajavuori & London
**When**: Thursday, March 12th from 14:00 onwards
**Other**: Dress appropriately for outdoor conditions

## Ticket sale: Linkki's 20th anniversary celebrations

**NOTE! The event itself, along with Kide.app ticket sale and gala website, are only in Finnish**

**NOTE! Ticket sale ends on 10.3. at 3pm!**

Link to the [Kide.app ticket sale](https://kide.app/events/b4e03f42-dcc6-4d08-be5f-9fff2482259e)

All Linkki members and alumni, along with their avecs, are welcome to attend the anniversary celebrations. 

The evening part of the anniversary celebrations will be held on Friday, March 20, 2026, in Jyväskylä at the Valtiontalo banquet hall in Kulttuurisuoja, where we will celebrate our student association in accordance with the traditions of academic banquets. The celebration will be followed by a traditional herring breakfast on the following day, Saturday, March 21, 2026, at the Juhlatalo Majakoski banquet hall. The herring breakfast ticket also includes transportation to the venue.

More info on the anniversary celebrations can be found [here](vujut.linkkijkl.fi)
Schedules and more detailed information can also be found in the event description on Kide.app.
