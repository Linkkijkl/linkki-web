## Dumppi's Vappuappro 15.4. from 4pm -> @ Downtown Jyväskylä

 VappuAppro is taking over Jyväskylä again on April 15, 2026! The interdisciplinary VappuAppro is a traditional student event where participants tour Jyväskylä’s restaurants and activities, collecting stamps in their activity pass and enjoying the May Day atmosphere together with friends. The event starts at Lounaispuisto, where event passes and after-party wristbands will be distributed in exchange for advance tickets from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM. The tour can be completed between 4:00 PM and 10:00 PM.

Once the activity pass is full, it is returned to the proctor at the after-party venues, Club Escape or London, where the party continues until the wee hours of the morning. In addition, Gloria serves as a non-after-party venue. A patch is awarded for a successful completion. Get ready for an unforgettable evening filled with student culture, fun, and May Day spirit—you won’t want to miss this!


**TL;DR;**
**WHAT?** Vappuappro
**WHERE?** Starts at Lounaispuisto, stops in downtown Jyväskylä
**WHEN?** April 15 at 4 p.m. ->
**WHY?** Because May Day just isn't May Day without an appro!

## Marathon sitsfest w/ Puolue, Mephisto, Fokus, Parku & Pedago 18.4. from 1pm -> @ Ilokivi Venue
Ready for the long run? Do sitsfests always end too early? Did your favourite song not make it? No longer!
Sitsfest should be a marathon, not a sprint.

We welcome you to Marathon sitsfest at Ilokivi Venue on April 18th from 1pm onwards. The sitsfest will be interdisciplinary and the following student organizations are participating: Fokus, Linkki, Mephisto, Parku, Pedago and Puolue. To make sure the theme doesn’t get boring Marathon sitsfest will be split into three different themes.

The themes are:
* Nowhere to wear
* Finnish memes
* Sports

The ticket includes entry to the whole marathon sitsfest, half a pizza, an overall patch and some snacks. The event is BYOB so bring your own drinks.

The event follows JYY’s safer space principles, and by attending you commit to following them. Equality officers will be present throughout the whole event and will be available if needed. An anonymous harassment contact form will also be made available.

**ACCESSIBILITY:** The premises of the Student House Ilokivi were renovated in 2017 to be accessible, and there is an elevator between the floors of the buildings with the assistance of staff. The restaurant on street level and the Venue downstairs both have a toilet with wheelchair access and grab bars.
