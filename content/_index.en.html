Welcome to Linkki's Homepage

Linkki Jyväskylä ry, more commonly known as Linkki, is a sizeable student association for Computer Science, Mathematical Information Technology, and Educational Technology majors and minors in University of Jyväskylä. Association was founded back in 2006 and it operates in the Faculty of Information Technology, within the Student Union of the University of Jyväskylä.

The purpose of the association is to promote its members' studies, hobbies, and common interests, as well as safeguard their rights as students. You can find more information in the rules section (in Finnish).

Linkki's membership is free and lifelong, and anyone studying the above-mentioned study programmes can apply. Membership allows you to enjoy, among other things, Linkki's membership benefits and free coffee at Kattila.
