## Vappusota w/ Algo 27.4. 5:30pm -> @ Megazone
Feeling annoyed, stressed, or just need to blow off some steam from the academic year? No worries — that’s exactly why we’re teaming up with Algo for a May Day battle! 💪

This year’s May Day battle will take place as a laser tag event at Megazone in Seppälä on Sunday, April 27th. The games start at 17:30, but please make sure to arrive at least 15 minutes early, i.e., by 17:15! The Megazone venue is reserved for our use for one hour. 🤩

If you haven’t tried laser tag before, no problem — the rules will be explained on-site, and all equipment is included in the ticket price. Just bring low-soled shoes such as sneakers or indoor sports shoes, a t-shirt (and preferably pants or a skirt), and a healthy competitive spirit! Dark clothing has been found to be a strategic advantage 👀

Equality representative: Anton Turpeinen (tg: @AntzaBoi)

TL;DR:
**What**: May Day battle! 🔫
**Where**: Megazone (Seppälä, Sorastajantie 1 A 5) 🏢
**When**: Mon 27.04 at 17:30 ☀️
**What to bring**: Comfortable (possibly dark) clothes and low-soled shoes 👟
**Other**: Please arrive at least 15 minutes before the games start! 🕓


## Sopokopo coffee appro 28.4. 10am - 3pm @ Association spaces
(Note: Sopo=social affairs, kopo=academic affairs)

Have you always wondered, what other student associations’ lounge rooms look like? Now you have a great opportunity to find out, as Ynnä, Nano, Syrinx, Pörssi, Dumppi, Linkki, Algo ja Fyysikkokerho unite their powers for Sopokopo coffee appro. It’s time to shift the veil of mystery and come visit other student associations’ spaces and enjoy some wellbeing themed activities!

Sopokopo coffee appro takes place in the aforementioned student association’s lounge rooms. Each room has a different activity that must be completed to gain a stamp on the appro pass. Like the name of the event suggests, there’s also coffee and small snacks available. A pass with five (5) or more stamps can be exchanged for an overall patch, but please do visit all eight locations if time allows! There’s no set schedule or route you have to take.

The appro is on Tuesday 28.4. from 10AM to 3PM. You may choose where you want to pick up your appro pass when signing up for the event.
The ticket sales open in kide.app on Monday 20.4. and end on Sunday 26.4. on the same week. The price of the ticket is 3 euros.

**WHAT**? Sopocopocoffees in appro form
**WHERE**? In student associations’ rooms at Agora, MaD and Ylistö
**WHEN**? 28.4. from 10AM to 3PM
**WHAT PRICE**? 3 euros
**WHY**? Having coffee is nice!
