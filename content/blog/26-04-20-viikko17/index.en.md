## Board games at Teerenpeli w/ Sane & Corpus 20.4. from 6pm -> @ Teerenpeli
Three amazing student associations are coming together to celebrate Vappu by organizing a board game night! What could be better? 🤩✨

The casual board game night organized by Sane, Corpus, and Linkki Jyväskylä ry is held at Teerenpeli on Monday, 20.4.2026 at 18 onwards! The event is free of charge, and all you need to bring is yourself to enjoy games and great company ❤️

Please note that the event follows JYY’s principles of a safer space. You can read more about them here:  [Jyväskylän yliopiston ylioppilaskunta](https://share.google/CrNUzFs87cC4bavf1)

The event also has equality representatives who will introduce themselves when the event begins. You can contact them by email at yhdenvertaisuus@sanery.org, corpus-hallitus@lists.jyu.fi or hyvinvointi@linkkijkl.fi  You can also tap their shoulder during the event! ❤️

Accessibility ❤️:
Teerenpeli is a bar located on Kauppakatu, and it is not fully accessible. There are raised platforms at the front door and when entering the back of the bar space, both of which can be moved over on a wheelchair with some assistance. Drinks can be served to tables in case moving to the bar counter is difficult. Some parts of the space may be narrow than others due to how tables are arranged, but there are also more spacious spots. There is an accessible women’s bathroom at the front of the bar that can be accessed at any point during the event. The lighting is static and warm, and the music is usually not too loud. There may be a mild smell of cigarettes, which may be uncomfortable for those that are sensitive to scents.

See you in Teerenpeli! ✨

Best regards,
Cone’s and Linkki’s event coordinators 💜💙🖤

TL;DR:

**WHAT**? Sane x Corpus x Linkki Jyväskylä ry Board game night ✨🔥
**WHERE**? Bar Teerenpeli, Kauppakatu 32
**WHEN**? Monday, 20.4.2026 at 18 onwards
**WHY**? It’s fun to play board games 🤩
**WHAT TO BRING**? Overalls (if you want) and good vibes! 🫶

## STEM wappu pesäpallo 26.4. 2pm -> @ Campus field
Matlu Pesäpallo Championship is here again!!! 
On Sunday, April 26 at 14:00, the campus field will once again host the battle to determine who really has control of the ball and a solid swing. 
In this lighthearted championship tournament, the teams competing are Ynnä, Linkki, Algo, Syrinx, Radikaali, and a joint team from Tiltti and Nano. The focus of the tournament is on good vibes and enjoying playing together. During the event, the winners of the “kostajaiset” will also be revealed, earning fame and glory. 
Anyone can join the games—no previous pesäpallo experience is required. There’s room on the field for both serious competitors and more casual, fun-focused players. If playing isn’t your thing, feel free to come watch, cheer, and enjoy the atmosphere. 
If you have your own pesäpallo equipment (gloves), it would be great if you could bring them along so everything runs smoothly and no games are held back due to lack of gear. 
There will be sausages grilling, provided by Loimu and TEK, and the spring campus setting offers the perfect frame for a shared Sunday ♨️☀️ 
 
TL;DR: 
**WHAT**: Matlu Pesäpallo Tournament 
**WHERE**: Campus field 
**WHEN**: Sunday, April 26 at 14:00 
**WHY**: Because sports and good vibes belong in the middle of Vappu 🎈
