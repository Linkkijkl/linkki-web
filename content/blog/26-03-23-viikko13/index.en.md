## Ynnän Appro 25.3. from 4pm -> @ Downtown Jyväskylä
Approhumu in the air…

SLURPS!

…the traditional Ynnä’s Appro is here again on March 25th, 2026! The start will once again be at Lounaispuisto, conveniently close to the bars and restaurants of the upper town. As always, participants will get to explore the bars of Jyväskylä city center, either individually or as a team of four. You can also choose which degree you want to complete—so if you’re feeling ambitious, why not go straight for the Tohtori? Oh wait!

 

This year you have a once in a lifetime opportunity, and I mean, ONCE IN A LIFETIME, to graduate as a Juhlatohtori. This year all participants who complete the Tohtori degree will graduate as a Juhlatohtori. Last time this was possible 30 years ago, and the next time tagain after 30 years in 2056.

 

But that’s not all! Participants can also take on fun bonus challenges along the way. There are four challenges in total, and each one earns you a unique patch—meaning you can collect up to five patches, or even six if you’re in a group!

The official afterparty will be at Club Escape, featuring a live artist performance. Entry is included in the ticket price.

**TL;DR:**
**What?** Ynnäs Appro
**Where?** Jyväskylä city center
**When?** Wednesday, March 25th
**Why?** Because Appro! 🍻
