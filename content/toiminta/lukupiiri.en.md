Linkki's book club gathers about once a month to discuss a chosen work. Gathering times and book suggestions are discussed in the [book club's telegram group](https://t.me/+SFwSBN8MrGNkMDA0). Gatherings have mostly been held on sundays at Musta Kynnys.

Our democratic process works as follows:
1. We choose the next criteria at the end of a gathering (e.g. genre, subject, type...)
2. Openly discuss book suggestions fitting the criteria (criteria fitness is for anyone to speculate)
3. We vote!
4. Independently reading the chosen work
5. We gather!

## Previously read works and their criteria
